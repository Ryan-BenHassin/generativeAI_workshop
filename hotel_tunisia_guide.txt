Hôtel Azur — A Tunisian Coastal Retreat

1. Overview
Hôtel Azur is a charming four-star hotel located on the Mediterranean coast of Tunisia, offering warm Tunisian hospitality, modern comforts, and easy access to local cultural sites. The property blends traditional Tunisian architecture with contemporary amenities and is ideal for leisure and business travelers.

2. Rooms & Suites
Rooms range from standard double rooms to family suites and a limited number of sea-view executive suites. Each room includes air conditioning, a private bathroom with hot water, complimentary Wi-Fi, a mini-fridge, a safe, and daily housekeeping. The executive suites offer additional living space and panoramic sea views.

3. Amenities
Amenities include a heated outdoor pool, a small fitness center, a spa offering massages and hammam treatments, an on-site business center, free Wi‑Fi in public areas, 24-hour front desk service, and complimentary shuttle service to nearby train and bus stations on request.

4. Dining
The hotel features a main buffet restaurant serving Tunisian and international cuisine for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. A la carte dining is available in the seaside restaurant with a focus on fresh seafood and Mediterranean flavors. There is also a lobby bar offering light snacks, coffee, and local teas.

5. Check-in, Check-out & Reservations
Standard check-in time: 14:00 (2:00 PM). Standard check-out time: 12:00 (noon). Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request subject to availability and may incur additional charges. Reservations are confirmed with a valid credit card; some rate types may require full prepayment.

6. Cancellation & Payment Policies
Cancellation policies vary by rate type. Flexible rates allow free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival. Non‑refundable rates require full payment at booking. Accepted payment methods include major credit cards; cash payments are accepted in local currency (Tunisian Dinar) at the front desk.

7. Location & Transport
Hôtel Azur is located approximately 25 km from Tunis–Carthage International Airport and 2 km from the nearest town center. The hotel offers an on-request airport transfer service (additional fee). Local taxis, bus routes, and a regional train station provide access to nearby historical sites and the capital, Tunis.

8. Nearby Attractions
Nearby attractions include the historic medina (old town), the ancient ruins of Carthage, Sidi Bou Said’s blue-and-white village, local beaches, and several coastal promenades. Day trips to the Sahara and inland olive groves can be arranged through the hotel concierge.

9. Accessibility
The property offers ground-floor rooms with step-free access and ramps to common areas. Not all rooms have roll-in showers; guests requiring specific accessibility features should contact the hotel prior to arrival to confirm room availability and accommodations.

10. Meetings & Events
Conference and small event facilities are available, including a meeting room that accommodates up to 60 attendees with basic AV equipment. Catering packages and bespoke event planning services can be arranged by the hotel events team.

11. Health, Safety & Sustainability
The hotel follows local health and safety guidelines, with routine cleaning, hand sanitizer stations, and staff training. For sustainability, Hôtel Azur participates in linen reuse programs, energy-efficient lighting, and local food sourcing when possible.
